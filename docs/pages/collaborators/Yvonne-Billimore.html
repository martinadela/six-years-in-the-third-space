Yvonne Billimore is an artist-curator, currently working as associate programme curator at Frame Contemporary Art Finland. Previously she worked at Scottish Sculpture Workshop in rural Aberdeenshire, where she developed and produced a programme of residencies, projects, workshops and public events. Her work facilitates situations for collective learning, exchange and experiences with particular attention given to feminist and ecological practices. She is currently exploring Feminist[i] Collective[ii] Research[iii] Practices[iv], with research performed through a range of approaches including archival research, workshops, reading groups, correspondence, writing and an open call for responses: fcrp.cargo.site/
