Diana Soria Hernandez (Mexico 1983) is a visual artist focused on the exploration of physical and corporeal language, mainly through performance art, live installation and drawing. She studied her BA in Fine Arts at ENPEG La Esmeralda in Mexico City, an MFA in Printmaking by the Academy of Fine Arts Helsinki and is pursuing a MA in Live Art and Performance Studies at the Theatre Academy Helsinki, both belonging to the University of the Arts, Helsinki. Her work has been shown internationally in exhibitions and festivals in Sweden, Finland, Norway, Denmark, Germany, Spain, Cyprus, England, USA, Mexico, Colombia, Thailand, India and China. Currently she lives and works mainly in Finland. She has received several working grants by Kone Foundation (2019-2020, 2017) and the Finnish Cultural Foundation (2018)
