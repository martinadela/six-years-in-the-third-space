Marten Esko

Marten Esko (b. 1990) works as curator, manager and writer in Tallinn. He holds an MA in Art History and Visual Culture from the Estonian Academy of Arts. He has been working with the Contemporary Art Museum of Estonia (EKKM) in Tallinn as an assistant curator since 2011, and as a managing director since 2016. He is also one of the board members of the Foundation Tallinn Print Triennial and one of the founders of NGO New Material, a non- periodical magazine publishing.
