Tina Mariane Krogh Madsen is an artist and researcher, who works in the intersection between performance art, sound, media, and matter. She is the founder of performance protocols, a platform for instruction-based art and collaborative processes. Madsen is educated from the College of Arts Crafts and Design (DK), holds a Master of Arts in Art History from Aarhus University (DK), and is currently a Doctoral Candidate at the Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture (FI).
