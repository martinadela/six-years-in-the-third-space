Venyvät huoneet / Stretchy Rooms
Juulia Terho & Milja-Maaria Terho

Stretchy Rooms (Venyvät huoneet, 2019) is a poem by Juulia Terho. It approaches the cloth and the act of dressing oneself as spaces and events that contain an opportunity for self-definition, play, and structuring one's own culture. The performativity of gender and the self become visible in rituals that in society are often considered vain and feminine. Stretchy Rooms has been previously presented as a collaborative performative piece that, among other spatial elements, consists of a video by Milja-Maaria Terho. Here the poem is for the first time published in a written format, and the video brought present in images that hint toward an aesthetic space that the text may inhabit.
